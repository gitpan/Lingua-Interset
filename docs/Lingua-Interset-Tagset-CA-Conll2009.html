NAME

Lingua::Interset::Tagset::CA::Conll2009 - Driver for the Catalan tagset of the CoNLL 2009 Shared Task.

VERSION

version 2.012

SYNOPSIS

  use Lingua::Interset::Tagset::CA::Conll2009;
  my $driver = Lingua::Interset::Tagset::CA::Conll2009->new();
  my $fs = $driver->decode("n\tpostype=common|gen=m|num=s");

or

  use Lingua::Interset qw(decode);
  my $fs = decode('ca::conll2009', "n\tpostype=common|gen=m|num=s");

DESCRIPTION

Interset driver for the Catalan tagset of the CoNLL 2009 Shared Task. CoNLL 2009 tagsets in Interset are traditionally two values separated by tabs. The values come from the CoNLL 2009 columns POS and FEAT.

Note that the ca::conll2009 and es::conll2009 tagsets are identical as they both come from the AnCora Catalan-Spanish corpus. For convenience, separate drivers called CA::Conll2009 and ES::Conll2009 are provided, but one is derived from the other.
